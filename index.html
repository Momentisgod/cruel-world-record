Technical Specs

Submission Requirements

Ensure your masters and assets meet platform standards before submission.

Audio Files

  • WAV format, uncompressed
  • 16-bit / 44.1kHz minimum
  • 24-bit / 48kHz+ for Apple Digital Masters
  • Dolby Atmos: ADM BWF format
  • No clipping — peak below 0 dBFS

Cover Artwork

  • 3000 × 3000 pixels minimum
  • JPG or PNG, RGB color space
  • No blurry or pixelated images
  • No social media handles or URLs
  • Apple Motion Art: .mov, H.264, 30fps

Metadata

  • Track titles — no ALL CAPS unless stylized
  • Artist names — consistent across releases
  • Genre and subgenre tags
  • ISRC codes (we generate if needed)
  • UPC/EAN codes (we generate if needed)
